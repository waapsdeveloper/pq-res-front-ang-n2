About the Berry Cafe
The MulBerry Fruit

The owner was inspired by the mulberry fruit trees and built a farm for health and longevity. All mixed drinks are made with fresh mulberries from the farm.
With the help of family and friends, the owner decided to bring happiness and health to the people in the community.

Our food in the cafe was specially crafted for all ages. We make sure they are well prepared, fresh and hygienic. We designed a cozy and comfortable dining experience. We listen to our customers and treat them like family.
